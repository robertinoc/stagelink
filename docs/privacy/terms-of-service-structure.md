# Terms of Service Structure

Status: StageLink-specific Terms of Service architecture. This is not final
legal copy.

## Required Sections

### 1. Introduction and Acceptance

State:

- StageLink provides artist pages, EPKs, links, analytics, integrations, and
  subscription features.
- By using StageLink, users accept the terms and privacy documents.
- Users must be legally able to enter into the agreement.

### 2. Eligibility and Accounts

Include:

- Account creators must be at least 18.
- Users must provide accurate account information.
- Users are responsible for their account access and team permissions.
- StageLink uses WorkOS/AuthKit for authentication.

### 3. Artist Pages, EPKs, and Public Content

Include:

- Users choose what content to publish.
- Public artist pages, EPKs, images, links, and contact details may be visible to
  anyone.
- Users must have rights to content they upload or link.
- Users are responsible for keeping booking/contact information accurate.
- StageLink may remove or disable content that violates the terms, law, security
  rules, or third-party rights.

### 4. User Responsibilities

Prohibit:

- Illegal activity.
- Harassment, hate, exploitation, or abusive content.
- Impersonation or misleading identity.
- Uploading malware or malicious files.
- Violating copyright, trademark, privacy, or publicity rights.
- Scraping, reverse engineering, or attacking the platform.
- Circumventing rate limits, auth, or access controls.
- Using StageLink to send spam or collect emails without proper consent.

### 5. Fan/Subscriber and Contact Data

State:

- Artists are responsible for lawful use of fan/subscriber data they collect
  through StageLink.
- Artists must honor unsubscribe/delete requests where applicable.
- Artists must not upload or collect data without permission.
- StageLink may provide tools to store consent evidence but does not make the
  artist's marketing compliant by default.

### 6. Integrations

Cover:

- Spotify, YouTube, SoundCloud, Shopify, Printful/Printify, Stripe, and other
  integrations are optional.
- Users authorize StageLink to access integration data only for chosen features.
- Third-party terms apply.
- StageLink may stop supporting an integration or require reauthorization.
- Users can disconnect integrations.

### 7. Subscriptions, Billing, and Plans

Include:

- FREE/PRO/PRO+ plan descriptions at a high level.
- Stripe processes payments.
- Billing cycles, cancellations, upgrades/downgrades.
- Taxes and payment failures.
- No guarantee of continued access to paid-only features after cancellation or
  non-payment.

Include:

- StageLink may evolve, suspend, or discontinue features.
- Integrations and third-party services may fail or change.
- Analytics may be estimates and may exclude bots/QA/internal traffic.
- StageLink does not guarantee uninterrupted service.

### 11. Termination and Suspension

Include:

- User-initiated cancellation/deletion.
- StageLink suspension for breach, risk, legal request, payment failure, or abuse.
- Effects on public pages, EPKs, assets, subscriptions, and retained records.

- Mandatory rights remain unaffected.
